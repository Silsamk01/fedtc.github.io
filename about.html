Federation of Data and Technology Council (FDTC)

FDTC as a Voice and Medium for Development

At FDTC, we serve as a powerful voice and an influential medium for fostering the continuous development of the data and technology industry. By providing a platform for thought leadership and innovation, we connect professionals, researchers, and organizations to encourage groundbreaking progress. We are dedicated to recognizing and celebrating great talent in data and technology, honoring those whose contributions inspire and shape the future of our field. Through this, FDTC plays a pivotal role in advancing not only the career trajectories of individuals but also the global technological landscape.

Mission Statement

To unite and inspire Data and IT professionals and researchers worldwide by fostering excellence, integrity, and innovation. Rooted in godly values that promote honesty, transparency, and ethical leadership, FDTC seeks to honor exceptional contributions, develop future experts, and advance the global impact of data and technology for the benefit of all.

Vision Statement

To be a globally respected association that drives meaningful advancements in data and technology, cultivating a diverse community of professionals committed to ethical innovation and lasting impact, guided by principles of integrity and service.

Objectives

  • Promote Excellence and Integrity: Celebrate and recognize exceptional achievements in data and technology while maintaining a foundation of honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Foster Professional Development: Provide mentorship, resources, and opportunities for growth, empowering professionals to excel in their careers and make meaningful contributions to the field.
  • Advance Ethical Innovation: Encourage groundbreaking research and responsible technological advancements that align with universal values of fairness, integrity, and respect for all.
  • Unite a Global Community: Build a welcoming platform for collaboration among professionals and researchers from diverse backgrounds, fostering innovation and addressing global challenges in data and technology.
  • Uphold Foundational Values: Strengthen the organization’s commitment to its foundational principles, rooted in godly values, which serve as a guide to ensure ethical and sustainable progress that benefits society at large.
