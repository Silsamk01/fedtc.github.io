Professional Qualifications

To be eligible for membership, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Must have a degree or equivalent professional qualification in a related field, such as:
    • Computer Science
    • Data Science
    • Information Technology
    • Cybersecurity
    • Software Engineering
    • Machine Learning or Artificial Intelligence
  • OR demonstrate exceptional contributions to the field, such as:
    • Published research papers
    • Development of innovative tools, technologies, or methodologies
    • Leadership in impactful tech initiatives

Evidence of Contribution

Applicants must provide evidence of contribution, such as:

  • Author of a book, research paper, or technical publication that has been used or cited in the field.
  • Developed or implemented technology that has improved systems or processes.
  • Actively mentoring or developing talent in the data and technology space.
  • Recognition by peers or organizations (awards, endorsements, certifications).

References

Provide two professional references, preferably from members of FDTC, industry leaders, or academic mentors who can attest to your qualifications, integrity, and impact.

Membership Tiers

FDTC offers tiered memberships to accommodate professionals at different stages of their careers:

  • Standard Member: Entry-level professionals or students meeting the minimum criteria.
  • Fellow Member: Senior professionals with 4+ years of experience or exceptional contributions to the field.
  • Honorary Member: Recognized leaders or innovators in the industry, admitted through nomination by FDTC leadership.
